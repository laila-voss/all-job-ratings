We asked Prolific participants to rate 105 occupations on 15 characteristics: 8 person-level characteristics describing the typical person in each job, and 7 occupation-level characteristics describing the job itself. Each rating was given on a 0-100 scale, where 0 means “much less than average” and 100 means “more than any other occupation.” Each respondent was randomly assigned 3 person-level and 2 occupation-level characteristics and rated 24 randomly-selected occupations.
